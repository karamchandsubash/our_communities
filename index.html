Why we do good


Community impact is in our DNA.

We made a commitment in 2015 to elevate our social impact. We hire people that care about their colleagues and their communities. Community impact is woven throughout our company culture, events, and everyday conversations.








Partners in impact


We can't revolutionize communities alone.

The communities we call home have given us a space to build our business, provided us with talented team members, and cheered us on along the way. We work to positively impact them like they’ve positively impacted us.

How We Do Good

Putting impactful action behind our commitment to good.


Paid Volunteer Time Off

Every employee is given 3 paid days off per year to donate their time to causes they care about.



Donation Matching

We support causes our employees care about financially through donation matching, depending on tenure we will match between $250 -$1000.



Events

We engrain Community Impact into our events throughout the year and semi-annual volunteer weeks with employees donating their time to every community we serve.



Community Leadership Council

In 2020, we created the Spreetail Community Leadership Council (SCLC). This cohort allows our employees to learn more about their communities while gaining valuable professional development.
